Goal

Pick up all the bacon and put it on the plate.

Controls

Movement

Use the arrow keys on your your keyboard to move around on the map.

Collect Bacon!

Walk over the bacon by using the arrow keys (see "Movement").

Plate Bacon!

Walk to the plate. Hit "Enter" to plate the bacon . Your glorious stack of plated bacon will grow proportionately to the amount of bacon you collected.

Troubleshooting

When I press the arrow keys, the window scrolls...

Either click in the game window or click on the "Play" tab. This should return focus to the game.

I have a Moo-bile device and want to play!

We currently do not support Moo-bile devices.
